SheetWise

Privacy
Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

SheetWise is a sheet-material cut optimizer. It is designed to keep your projects and data private by default. SheetWise does not sell personal data and does not use personal data for advertising or tracking.

Information SheetWise Uses

Projects and pieces. SheetWise stores the projects, sheet dimensions, piece lists, quantities, labels, kerf settings, material costs, templates, and app preferences you create or edit in the app.

App preferences. SheetWise stores your unit preference (metric or imperial) using your device's local app storage.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device in your app's Documents directory. SheetWise does not use accounts, cloud sync, or remote servers. Your projects never leave your device unless you explicitly export them.

Export

SheetWise lets you export your cut layouts as PDF files. Exported files are created only when you choose to export them, and you control where those files are saved or shared.

Network Requests

SheetWise makes no network requests. It does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking.

Children

SheetWise is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated as SheetWise changes. If the app adds new data collection, sync, or account features, this policy will be reviewed and updated.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@jusder.me.
