What it does

Optimized Layouts

Packs your pieces onto sheets with minimal waste using an efficient bin-packing algorithm.

📄

Templates

Save your standard sheet sizes and material types as reusable templates. Start any project in seconds.

📦

Multi-Sheet Support

Automatically spans pieces across as many sheets as needed when one isn't enough.

💰

Cost Tracking

Enter the price per sheet and see your total material cost update in real time.

📷

PDF Export

Export a clean, print-ready cut sheet PDF to take to the shop or share with a fabricator.

🌍

Metric & Imperial

Toggle between metric and imperial units at any time. Works the way you work.
