In Development

What it does

🚀

Gamified Spirometry

Blow into a spirometer and watch a rocket rise in real time. Hitting your goal sends it to the stars — therapy that feels like play.

🎯

Personalized Goals

Each patient has a target lung volume in mL, adjusted as they improve. Sessions track reps, streaks, and personal bests automatically.

👥

Multi-Patient Profiles

A single device handles an entire ward. Switch between patients instantly, each with their own history, goals, and progress.

📋

Clinician Controls

PIN-protected nurse settings for adjusting goals, calibrating the device, and reviewing session data — invisible to patients.

📡

Device Connectivity

Connects to standard spirometers over Bluetooth or USB Serial. Works with off-the-shelf medical hardware already on wards.

📱

iOS Companion App

A companion iPad app for nurses to manage profiles, review session history, and hand off to the next shift without touching the patient device.
