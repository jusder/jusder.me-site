Pick Me Up makes it easy to organize and join community trash pickups in Denver. Coordinate events, track your impact, and keep your city clean.

In Development

What it does

📅

Community Events

Find and join local trash pickup events happening near you in Denver.

🎉

Event Hosting

Create and share pickup events with your neighborhood in just a few taps.

📊

Impact Tracking

See the collective difference your community is making over time.

📍

Route Planning

Map out efficient pickup routes for your corner of the city.

🌏

Neighborhood Map

See active and upcoming cleanups pinned on a live map of your neighborhood.

📚

Cleanup History

Log your pickups and track your personal contribution to a cleaner city over time.
